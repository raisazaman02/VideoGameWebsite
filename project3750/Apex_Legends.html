Facts About Apex Legends

  1. Apex Legends is a battle royale game that starts with 100 players and ends with one player who is crowned the winner.
  2. Recently they added ways Gamers can choose the mode they would like to play in.
  3. You can add friends and meet new people and create the best team.
  4. Like Fortnite gamers can help their teammates by joining the voice chat built into the game.
  5. There is a variety of characters to choose from, with different skills.
  6. Apex has seasons and ever season the game introduces new legends.
  7. Apex Legends Battle Pass and introduced more legends, weapons, and loot.
