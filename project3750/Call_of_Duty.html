COD: Call Of Duty

Call of duty is an action game that shows you how combat can progress. The game follows combat soldiers and focuses on how different groups of people can impact the war. The game was developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It was released on October 29, 2003. IW engine was used to create the game. The IW engine is a game engine developed by Infinity Ward and Treyarch for this series. The engine was originally based on id Tech 3 as its core, since the engine itself is proprietary with inclusion of GtkRadiant by id Software.
