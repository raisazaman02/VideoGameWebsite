The game will give you an experience of hell, a first-person survival horror game developed by veterans. You are an investigative journalist Miles Upshur, explore Mount Massive Asylum. The objective is to try to survive long enough to discover its terrible acts taken place in the asylum. The game starts as you enter the Asylum and see all the patients with certain parts surgically removed. You follow the blood and find clues to what happened inside the Asylum. The character has a camera that can be used to see in the dark. When things are chasing you, you are allowed to hid in the barrels. The character gets bruised up along the way, but it is a great game to ramp up your heart. Outlast was created by Red Barrels. They used Unreal Engine 3, which is a suite of well built tools for game developers to design and build games, and visualizations.
