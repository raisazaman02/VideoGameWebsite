What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is a battle royale game that starts with 100 players and ends with one player who gets the victory royale. Gamers can choose the mode they would like to play in, solo, duo mode or in a squad. They can add friends to their squad and play as a team. The game's animations and graphics are some reasons people play this game. Gamers can help their teammates by joining the voice chat built into the game.

Season 1

Season one was the first Fortnite season. The games wore free default skins or purchased the new skins from the item shop. The season was essential, with no daily challenges giving the players the time to get used to the controls. The item shop needed to be unlocked before you made a purchase.

Season 2

Season two is when the Battle Pass came into the picture. Players were beginning to learn more and more about the games and the beautiful map. The first series of big map changes included Shifty Shafts, Snobby Shores and Tilted Towers. Tilted Towers quickly became the most active city in all of Fortnite. The battle pass was here, which made games play more to level up. If you hit tier 70 of the Battle Pass, you unlocked the Black Knight.

Season 3

Season three was when Fortnite gained the most popularity. The gameplays improved, and players starting to find new techinques to enhance their chance to win. Lucky Landing was introduced to celebrate the Chinese New Year.

Season 4

This season was superhero-themed. Players had to unlock the fully upgraded Omega outfit using the challenges in the battle pass. After Dusty Depot was destroyed, it left behind Dusty Divot. The new map locations were added, including Risky Reels. The Jetpack was added but was removed shortly after. The Drum Gun was added but later changed to make it less overpowered. Dual Pistols and Stink Bombs and Stink Bombs were also added. “Blast Off!” was the name of the end of season event.

Season 5 - Season X

From season 5-X we saw more battle pass changes and more changes to the map. New guns were introduced and old ones were taking out. The map constantly shifted and more new tequeniques were prefected. Now, Fortnite is on to the next chapter of the famous games.
